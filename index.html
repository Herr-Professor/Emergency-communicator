Emergency Communication

Request Help

If you are injured, trapped, or in danger, please fill out this form to request help. We will try to connect you with the nearest medical personnel or rescue team as soon as possible.

Offer Help

If you are a volunteer, a donor, or a service provider, and you want to offer help to the affected people, please fill out this form to register your details. We will try to match you with the most suitable requests for help.

For urgent assistance, please contact local emergency services.
